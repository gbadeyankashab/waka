How does it work?

Wakanow Pay Small Small (PSS) is a flexible travel payment plan that allows customers lock down great travel deals by making as little as 25% down payment of the total travel cost and paying the rest in convenient instalments. PSS allows you to save as much as 150% by taking advantage of the ‘early bird’ fares, and spread your payment over a convenient period of time.

Frequently Asked Questions

I opted for a 1month tenor but my travelling date is in 4 months, can I defer my payment towards my travel date

Yes your repayment plan can be restructured and the rates applicable to the new tenor will apply

I have not completed my payment but I have paid above the down payment. How much do I get back as refund if I decide to cancel my ticket?

You will get the amount in excess of the 25% minimum down payment or cancellation charges

I have completed my payment but I need to cancel the ticket. How do I get my refund?

Send an email to PSS@wakanow.com you will be credited with the refund value

I have completed my payment but I am unable to travel. Can I leave my ticket open to be used at a future date?

Yes, you can leave your ticket open and use later. This must be within the validity period of the ticket.

I am unable to make my next instalment on the agreed repayment date.

Failure to abide by the agreed payment terms will attract a default fee and subsequent cancellation after a 30 day default period.

How do I know my reservation was booked?

You will receive an email on confirmation of your booking with Wakanow

I am travelling in 8 months’ time; can I make my booking under the Scheme now? How long can I pay back?

Yes, you can book ahead of your trip under the Pay Small Small Scheme. Our tenor ranges from 2 weeks to 6 months.

Is there any Service Charge added to my fare if I opt for the Pay Small Small payment option.

Yes, Service Charges are applicable on all Pay Small Small bookings;

How do I know my reservation was booked?

You will receive an email on confirmation of your booking with Wakanow;

How do I know my next payment due date for the Pay Small Small?

A notification email will be sent to you 48 hours before the due date.

Can I use my Debit/Credit card to make payments for Pay Small Small?

Yes

Can a customer increase his initial down payment?

Yes, while making a booking, you will be required to select your preferred down payment

When do I get my ticket?

Ticket is issued immediately we receive your down payment. However, this will only be released to you upon confirmation of final payment as advised on your payment schedule.

If I default in payment do I get back my Down Payment?

A minimum of 25% of the total transaction value is non-refundable (subject to cancellation charges). You will only get a refund if down payment made is more than 25%.

Does the Pay Small Small payment option allow cancellation or change in travel date?

Yes, changes are permitted on PSS transactions. Rules of the ticket will apply as regards refunds and cancellations.

Is Pay Small Small applicable to all ticket types?

Yes! PSS is applicable to all ticket types as long they are up to the minimum transaction value

Is PSS applicable to flight only? PSS is applicable to flight and hotel What is the minimum amount required to qualify?

The minimum amount required to qualify for Pay Small Small Scheme is N100,000 for flight and N50,000 for hotel

I’m trying to login but I keep getting a message saying “Unable to Login”, what does this mean?

This may occur when the wrong user name or/and password is inputted. Contact pss@wakanow.com for your user I.D and password reset

Can I Complete my payment after my travel date?

No. All payments for PSS transactions should be completed before the travel date

Do I qualify for Pay Small Small?

Pay Small Small is open to all Wakanow Customers

Terms & Conditions

1.     All installments must be paid in full before the travel date, according to the selected payment options.

2.     All transactions made on the “Pay Small Small” scheme are subject to a minimum 25% down payment and service charges. (payable upfront and non-refundable)

3.     Subsequent installments will be made via direct debit on the same card for down payments made with the card. This means that Wakanow has the authority to subsequently debit the card-linked account automatically for the balance after the initial deposit.

4.     Flight tickets and Hotel reservations will be locked down upon initial down payment. A lien will be placed on the ticket until all installments are paid in full.

5.     Date Change is permitted. Customers will be required to pay the difference in fare and applicable charges.

However, Changes would only be effected when full payments have been confirmed.

6.     Customer is prohibited from utilizing hotel reservations until all installments are paid in full.

7.     Default Policy;

            i.       Default on the agreed payment plan will attract a 5% penalty charge

           ii.       In case of default and expiration of loan tenor, a Tenor modification rule (Payment date would be moved to the closest applicable tenor which would exceed original travel date where new tenor rates would be applicable) would apply

            iii.         iii. Ticket will be canceled 3 days to the travel date or after a 30day grace period (whichever is closer)

8. Refund policy:   

i. For a refundable ticket, the down payment will be utilized to pay all cancellation fees for the flight or hotel.

However, if the cancellation fees exceed the down payment, the relevant deductions will be made from the amount paid before refund.

           ii. For a non-refundable ticket, the customer shall not be entitled to any refund in the event of cancellation.

9.     Travel Insurance policy on Pay Small Small is not confirmed until final payment.

10.  Tickets will not be released BEFORE payment of the final installment to procure Traveler’s Allowance (PTA or BTA).
